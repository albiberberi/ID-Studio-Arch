About us

Founded in 1995 and headquartered in Brussels, iD Studio Projects is a multidisciplinary practice driven by a holistic approach to the built environment. Since our inception, we have operated on the founding principle that true innovation emerges from the synergy of complementary disciplines.

Our strength lies in our diverse collective of professionals. Under the guidance of our partners, this team applies a rigorous, research-based methodology to complex projects of all scales. From residential developments and educational facilities to large-scale urban revitalizations and international development initiatives, iD Studio Projects manages the entire lifecycle of a project. We are committed to "complete missions," ensuring excellence from the first sketch to final delivery, both within Europe and in our development cooperation work abroad.
